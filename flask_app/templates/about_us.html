Our Mission

To make robotics education an accessible and enjoyable experience. The platform has been designed to streamline and enhance the learning experience for undergraduate students interested in and wanting to dip their feet into the fields of robotics or to further master the fundamentals of robotics.

OUR VISION

Creating a future through accessible robotics education

We aim to build a future where every student—regardless of background or resources—has the opportunity to learn about robotics. We envision a world where robotics education thrives in a virtual learning environment. This project is designed to provide undergraduate students with a virtual robotics lab. Students will be able to learn about coding robotics simulations, apply what they've learned through quizzes and exams, and code in a live coding environment. Our platform will empower the next generation to shape the future of robotics, one simulation at a time.
