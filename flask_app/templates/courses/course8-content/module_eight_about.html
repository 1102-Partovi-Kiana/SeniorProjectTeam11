Fetch Stack Robot

The Fetch-Stack robot is designed to use what students have learned about the robots with the integration of stacking. The Fetch-Organize robot is equipped with several joints and actuators, allowing it to perform basic movements. The goal of this robot is to pick up and stack objects on top of one another.
