About this course

Robots are everywhere, changing the way we live and work. This course introduces you to different types of robots—like industrial, medical, mobile, and humanoid—and shows how they’re used in real life. From industrial robots revolutionizing manufacturing to medical robots saving lives, you'll learn the intricacies being these machines. This course offers valuable insights into the technology driving innovation today.
