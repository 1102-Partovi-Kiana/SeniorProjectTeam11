Fetch Organize Robot

The Fetch-Organize robot is designed to use what students have learned about the robots with the integration of color based organization. The Fetch-Organize robot is equipped with several joints and actuators, allowing it to perform basic movements. The goal of this robot is to identify objects, grasp them, and place them in the correct container.
