Fetch Robot with Sensors

The Fetch-Organize-With-Sensors robot is designed to use what students have learned about the robots with the integration of sensors. The Fetch-Reach robot is equipped with several joints and actuators, allowing it to perform basic movements. The goal of this robot is to reach the destination point with the tip of the robot.
